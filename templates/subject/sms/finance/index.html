How to use Finance & Assets

1. Set up finance categories

Use Finance categories to define the main income and expense lines for the school:

  • Income: School fees, Hall hire, Donations, …
  • Expenses: Salaries – GB staff, Electricity, Stationery, …
  • Mark School fees as “fee income” so it links to learners.

2. Capture income & expenses

Use the Cashbook to record everything:

  • Fee payments per learner (cash, EFT, stop orders).
  • Other income such as hall hire, fundraising, donations.
  • Expenses such as salaries, municipal accounts, stationery, etc.
  • Use the bank / receipt reference so you can match cashbook to bank statements later.

3. Review the year

The Income statement & fees helper will summarise:

  • Total fee income vs other income.
  • Main expense lines for the school year.
  • Simple views to help the SGB decide fees for the next year.

4. Assets & stock (coming later)

A future module will allow simple tracking of textbooks, devices, furniture and other school resources:

  • Room-level inventories (e.g. Grade 7A classroom).
  • Issue / return records for learners and staff.
  • Flags for lost or damaged items for SGB reporting.
