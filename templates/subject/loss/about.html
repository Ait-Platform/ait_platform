The Loss of a Loved One

A Snapshot of My Journey from that Tragic Moment to Now,
Measured by My Adaptation Vector.

Grief is deeply personal and non-linear. While many tools describe stages of grief, this program offers something more meaningful: a way to reflect on your Coping Journey — the path you've taken from the moment of your loss to where you are now.

At the heart of this journey is a structured model we call the Adaptation Vector. It helps us measure not just where you are emotionally, but how you’ve moved through time — capturing the direction and strength of your adaptation.

You’ll answer a set of yes/no questions designed to surface patterns in your grief response. Your answers create a snapshot of your emotional progression — no judgment, just awareness.
