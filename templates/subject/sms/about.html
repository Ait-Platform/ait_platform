School Management System (SMS)

A practical, African school management system built for real school life: governing bodies, learners, teachers, finance, and assets in one place.

SGB & Compliance

Keep a clean record of SGB members, meetings, minutes, and resolutions, so you are always prepared for audits and legal requirements.

Learners & Classes

Admissions, grades, promotions, and learner records in one secure register that follows each learner year by year.

Teachers & Staff

A proper register of teachers, subjects, phases, and responsibilities, including specialist roles and class allocations.

Finance & Assets

Track fees, payments, arrears, and an assets register for textbooks, equipment, and stock-taking control.

Ready to onboard your school?

The SMS runs on the same AIT platform as your other programmes, but is dedicated to your school as an institution.

Start SMS registration

You will first create an AIT account for the school (principal or delegated admin). Once SMS is activated, you will access it via the School Management dashboard.
