Loss Continuum Assessment (LCA)

You are about to begin a short diagnostic test. The purpose of this test is to assess your present position on the continuum of emotional responses following the loss of a loved one.

You are required to answer each item truthfully and without hesitation. There are no right or wrong answers. The results will be used to provide feedback regarding your current grief stage.

You will be presented with a series of statements. Please respond to each with a clear Yes or No. The test is designed to be completed in one sitting. Do not pause unless instructed.
